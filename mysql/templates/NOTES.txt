MySQL has been deployed successfully!

To connect to your MySQL database:

1. Get the root password:

  export MYSQL_ROOT_PASSWORD=$(kubectl get secret --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} {{ .Values.secret.existingSecret }} -o jsonpath="{.data.mysql-root-password}" | base64 -d)

2. Connect via port-forward:

  kubectl port-forward --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} svc/{{ include "mysql.fullname" . }} 3306:3306 &
  mysql -h 127.0.0.1 -P 3306 -uroot -p$MYSQL_ROOT_PASSWORD
